Software Engineer

Job Description


Software engineering is a job that involves designing and building software applications such as video games, buisness applications, operating systems, network controls and much more. It is a job that requires problem solving skills, as well as a lot of coding and programming experience. A software engineers salary can range anywhere between $74,057 to $188,233, but the average salary is $118,067 per year. The beginners with less than 1 year of experince hav their average salary just at $110,324. This job requires you to have at least a bachelors degree in a computer science discipline, mainly because of all the computer science skills required. This I a job that I want to do because of all the coding and the problem solving, both of which I enjoy.
